About Me

The one word that encompasses the entirety of this man’s life: NERD. Finding his curiosity for computers and technology at the young age of 5, he and his older sister tried their hand at programming using an old IBM Portable Computer, a game coding book, and a red 5 1/2" floppy copy of Basic. After spending 2 weeks meticulously copying the code line by line, they tried to start their own copy of "Marco Polo". The game failed to start due to a syntax error somewhere in the 394 lines of code. It wouldn’t be until Kris was 29 that he rediscovered that love of the dark background of the technological world.
